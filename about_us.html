About Us

Alchemy by Queen's is the brainchild of Teena Naz, a second-generation beauty entrepreneur. Her mother, Thahira Nizar, pioneered beauty services in Kannur, a city famous for its beautiful people, when she launched Queen's in 1990. Teena grew up watching her mother transform women with her craft of enhancing beauty. Teena honed her eye for the beautiful further over the years and studied multiple aspects of what makes beauty. With Alchemy, she is razor-focused on one mission: help women discover beautiful skin and gorgeous hair. She envisions Alchemy as the cordon bleu of beauty solutions in the city. Queen's has a legacy of valuing the customer above all and Alchemy takes that philosophy strides further.
